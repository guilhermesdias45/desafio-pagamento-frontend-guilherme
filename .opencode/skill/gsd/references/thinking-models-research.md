# Thinking Models for Research

## Socratic Questioning

For every claim, ask:
- What evidence supports this?
- What contradicts this?
- What are the underlying assumptions?
- What would change my confidence?

## Steel-manning

Argue the opposing view strongly:
- Best case for each alternative
- Honest assessment of trade-offs
- Prevents confirmation bias

## Fermi Estimation

Quick order-of-magnitude estimates:
- Break into estimable components
- Use ranges, not point estimates
- Combine with geometric mean
- Validate against known benchmarks
